EnPoWER

Overview

"EnPoWER- Engineering Oriented Promotion of Work Experience and Research, is a student-driven body under the UGAC (Undergraduate Academic Council), dedicated to promote the research culture of IIT Bombay. Our primary objective is to bridge the gap between students with industries and professors, and help them explore various research and industry opportunities that align with their interest. We work to provide students with access to resources related to core-related research during integral points in their lives like resume-making, grad school applications, university internships, etc. We also organize events to ignite curiosity among undergraduates and let them explore it further with events like SURP, ResCon, Enthuse and so on. Our aim is to promote a healthy and sustaining research community at IIT Bombay and support them with essential resources that will assist them in pursuing their dreams."
