Divisions

Analytics Club

The Analytics Club, IIT Bombay fosters interest in Data Science, ML, and AI through workshops, sessions, hackathons, and industry ties, empowering students with skills for the evolving analytics world.

Consult Club

The Consult Club is the hub for all things consulting, strategy, and business problem-solving at IIT Bombay. It aims to foster analytical thinking, structured problem-solving, and industry readiness through speaker sessions, workshops, case competitions, and live projects. The club empowers students with essential skills for careers in consulting and management while building a collaborative and growth-oriented community.

Finance Club

Finance Club, IIT Bombay empower students to explore finance through hands-on workshops, expert sessions, and industry tie-ups across investment banking, private equity, quant, and more. With a focus on practical learning and peer-driven growth, we aim to make finance accessible, engaging and career-shaping for all.

EnPoWER

EnPoWER fosters the research culture among students. We are the bridge between students, industries, and the professors. We support and help students explore various research related opportunities through our various programs and events like ResCon, SURP, WURP, Hackathons and much more

SSS

Student Support Services (SSS) at IIT Bombay supports students academically through tutorials, language assistance, faculty-student engagement, UG TA selections, and updated online resources, ensuring a smooth academic journey for undergraduates.

Career Cell

The Career Cell at IIT Bombay empowers students by providing comprehensive information about each step in an IITian's career journey, helping them overcome uncertainties, and guiding them to make informed decisions for a successful professional future.

CCG

The aim of CCG is to foster a strong consulting culture at IIT Bombay by solving real-world problems, mentoring students, and enabling hands-on exposure to consulting and strategy-building.

Investment Team

To unite finance enthusiasts on a journey of continuous learning, innovation and exploration in finance to challenge conventional norms to create a lasting learning experience.

DAV

The Data Analytics and Visualization Team empowers students with data-driven tools, insights, and dashboards to make informed academic decisions and navigate their academic journey confidently and efficiently.

ISIR

The IR Office at IIT Bombay manages international collaborations, student/faculty exchanges, MoUs, government and embassy relations, and promotes global engagement through research, exchanges, and language courses.
